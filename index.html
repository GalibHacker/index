Sovereign ratings of Bangladesh

Bangladesh achieves Ba3(Moody's) and BB-(Standard and Poor's) with stable outlook for the 13th consecutive years . Fitch Ratings affirmed BB- with stable outlook for the 9th time.

100 years of Mujib

The government of Bangladesh has announced the commemoration of 2020–2021 as the Mujib Year on the occasion of the centennial birth anniversary of the founding leader of the country, Sheikh Mujibur Rahman. This year will be celebrated from March 17, 2020 to March 31, 2022.
